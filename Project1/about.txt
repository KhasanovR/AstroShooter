This project is concerning on entertainment. Actually, when you run the 
software  it  will  ask you load existing profile or create new profile,
which  means  that  you  can  all your data and use it when you feel it 
necessary. Frankly, This game  is about a situation like Armageddon and 
you can become a hero of the earth. During the game you will store rate, 
level, and you will have three  lives which means your spaceship can be 
damaged only three times. If more than three times asteroids damage the 
spaceship game is over  and  this  is the end of the mankind and planet 
earth. And you can  watch top rate and level if you want to see.
